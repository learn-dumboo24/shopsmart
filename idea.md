# ShopSmart 

## Project Overview

ShopSmart is an e-commerce web application designed to provide users with a smooth and secure online shopping experience. As part of this college project, the goal is to **extend and modify the existing ShopSmart project** by adding meaningful, practical, and innovative features that demonstrate individual understanding, creativity, and real-world problem solving.

This modified version focuses on **smart shopping assistance, personalization, sustainability, and better user experience**, while keeping the core structure of the original project intact.

---

## Motivation Behind the Idea

Most e-commerce platforms today focus only on selling products, not on helping users make *better* decisions. Users often:

* Buy unnecessary items
* Miss better alternatives
* Overspend due to lack of insights
* Have no awareness of eco-friendly choices

The idea behind enhancing ShopSmart is to transform it from **just an online store** into a **smart shopping assistant** that helps users shop wisely, efficiently, and responsibly.

---

## Proposed Enhancements (My Modifications)

### 1. Smart Product Recommendation System

* Recommend products based on:

  * User search history
  * Previously viewed products
  * Category preferences
* Display a "You may also like" section on product pages
* Simple logic-based recommendation (no heavy ML required)

Purpose: Improves user engagement and mimics real-world e-commerce behavior.

---

### 2. Budget-Aware Shopping Feature

* Allow users to set a **monthly or session-based budget**
* Show warnings when cart value exceeds the budget
* Display remaining budget dynamically

Purpose: Encourages mindful spending and adds a unique practical feature.

---

### 3. Eco-Friendly Product Indicator

* Add tags like:

  * "Eco-Friendly"
  * "Recyclable Packaging"
  * "Low Carbon Footprint"
* Provide a filter for eco-friendly products only

Purpose: Promotes sustainability and aligns with modern ethical shopping trends.

---

### 4. Price Comparison (Internal)

* Show price comparison between similar products available **within ShopSmart**
* Highlight the best-value product

Purpose: Helps users make informed decisions without leaving the platform.

---

### 5. Wishlist & Save for Later

* Users can save products to a wishlist
* Option to move items from cart to "Save for Later"

Purpose: Improves usability and user retention.

---

### 6. Enhanced Order Tracking System

* Step-based order tracking:

  * Order Placed
  * Packed
  * Shipped
  * Out for Delivery
  * Delivered
* Visual progress bar

Purpose: Makes order status clear and user-friendly.

---
